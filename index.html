About

OD is an acronym for the phrase "On Duty". It is usually applied by students of an institution or faculties sometimes, to explain the reason for their absence on a specific period of time (an hour/half day/full day) inside the campus or class. It cannot be applied or retrieved after being absent. To be short, you are informing someone that you would be on leave for so-and-so duration prior to it!

Essentials

You will be asked to enter the following credentials of yours. Before submitting the form just make sure that every single credential given by you is to the best of your knowledge.

    Name
    Register number
    Department
    Year
    Section
    Number of days(if less than 24 hours, mention duration)
    From-and-To date
    A valid REASON

Guidelines

The student/staff has to submit the prior permission letter for granting ON DUTY (OD) (for varius reasons viz., attending conferences, STTPs, workshops, symposiums, SSG activities, etc...) through the Faculty Advisor (FA) to the Academic Section. The student/staff will be informed once the approval is obtained from the Competent Authority. Upon return, the duly-filled application has to be submitted to the Academic Section within THREE working days from the date of return along with endorsement of Faculty Advisor (FA)
